A lab bench that fits in your hand.

Dalton turns chemistry into a direct manipulation experience: place atoms, form bonds, validate structures, and learn by making.

Build freely

Use the sandbox canvas to arrange atoms, connect bonds, adjust charges, and see formulas emerge as structures become stable.

Discover compounds

Guided challenges invite learners to assemble molecules and grow a personal discovery history.

Explore elements

Browse an element library with shells, valence behavior, category colors, masses, and quick reference data.

Convert structures

Work with SMILES and InChI codes for chemistry workflows that bridge classroom sketches and real identifiers.

Local-first learning

Core chemistry data and saved progress live on the device, with graceful offline behavior when network services are unavailable.

Public data lookup

When online, Dalton can use chemistry services and PubChem to help parse, validate, and enrich molecular structures.
